This assessment is for informational purposes only and does not constitute a clinical diagnosis. It combines the Gotland Male Depression Scale (Zierau et al., 2002), validated for paternal depression, and a partner observation adaptation of the Edinburgh Postnatal Depression Scale (Cox et al., 1987). A significant score indicates that professional assessment is warranted - not that a diagnosis has been made.
