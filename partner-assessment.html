This assessment is for informational purposes only and does not constitute a clinical diagnosis. It combines the Gotland Male Depression Scale (Zierau et al., 2002), validated for paternal depression, and a partner observation adaptation of the Edinburgh Postnatal Depression Scale (Cox et al., 1987), validated for perinatal mood screening. A significant score indicates that professional assessment is warranted - not that a diagnosis has been made. If you are concerned about immediate safety, contact a GP or call the Samaritans on 116 123 (free, 24/7) or CALM on 0800 58 58 58.
